Distributed to charities selected by the Trustee.
Ong Lai Mun Charity Fund is a charitable trust, a set of assets left by Ong Lai Mun based on a note written that indicates to the trustee named Kuan Soo Huey, for the charity of her choice. The assets are held and managed by the trustee since 2019, with some or all interest that the assets produce going to the fund. The Fund does not accept any external donations and all the record of the balance is published officially on this website.

The distribution of the fund shall last through the lifetime of the trustee. As according to Ong Lai Mun's verbally told the idea about not giving all the lum sum, but little by little over long period of time to make minimal impact but long and sustainable duration.

The trustee shall follow, not strictly, on the rule, 70% to charities or needy individuals in Malaysia, 20% to Cancer related chairities in Singapore (for the reason that Ong Lai Mun worked and received cancer treatment in Singapore), and 10% to global charities.
